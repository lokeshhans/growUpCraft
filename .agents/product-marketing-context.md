# GrowUpCraft

## Brand Overview

GrowUpCraft is a modern web development agency focused on building high-performance React.js websites for schools, coaching institutes, startups, and local businesses in India.

The company specializes in fast, SEO-optimized, mobile-responsive websites designed to increase online visibility, improve trust, and generate business leads.
